# Croatian

Support `hr-HR`.

## Quotation marks

Use `„Primjer”` for an outer quotation and `‘primjer’` for a nested quotation.
Follow the Croatian source convention for adjacent punctuation:

- If quoted words form only part of the surrounding sentence, close the quote
  before the sentence period.
- If the quotation is a complete sentence introduced as such, keep its final
  punctuation inside the closing quotation mark.

## Dash and range rules

- Distinguish the hyphen (`spojnica`) from the dash (`crtica`).
- Use a spaced dash for a numeric range in prose in the official Croatian
  convention: `18 – 20 godina`. Preserve an established compact style for
  references such as page ranges.
- Do not combine the range dash with an explicit `od … do` construction. Choose
  either `2009. – 2011.` or the corresponding words, not both.
- Preserve an established editorial dash style for parenthetical interruptions
  and dialogue.

## Other conventions

- Put no space before `, . : ; ? !`.
- Preserve Croatian diacritics: `č`, `ć`, `đ`, `š`, `ž`.
- Use curly apostrophes only where a name, omission, or cited foreign form
  requires one.
- Delegate numbers, euro, percentages, dates, times, units, lists, collation,
  and plurals to `hr-HR` locale APIs.
