# Swedish

Support `sv-SE`.

## Quotation marks

Swedish commonly uses the right double quotation mark on both sides:
`”Exempel”`. Use the right single quotation mark on both sides for a nested
quotation: `’exempel’`.

Do not replace the opening mark with an English left double quote merely
because a smart-quotes tool expects asymmetric pairs. Preserve punctuation
according to whether it belongs to the quotation or the surrounding sentence.

## Other conventions

- Put no space before `, . : ; ? !`.
- Use a spaced en dash (`tankstreck`) for an interruption or dialogue when the
  editorial style calls for it. Use a hyphen for compounds and word-level joins.
- Keep ranges consistent and distinguish the range dash from a minus sign.
- Use a curly apostrophe only for names, omissions, or forms that require it;
  Swedish genitives ordinarily do not take an apostrophe.
- Keep numbers with adjacent units and symbols when required by the output.
- Delegate numbers, Swedish kronor, dates, times, units, and lists to `sv-SE`
  locale APIs.
