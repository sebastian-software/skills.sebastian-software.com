# Lead Magnet and Funnel

Add a funnel only when the commercial thesis and useful first step are clear.
A funnel cannot repair an irrelevant offer; it can only expose the mismatch
faster.

## Define the lead magnet

Create an asset or experience that helps the target buyer make a real first
step while revealing a larger problem the business can credibly solve.

Specify:

- exact buyer and triggering situation
- immediate problem and desired first result
- format chosen for utility, not perceived volume
- narrow promise and concrete title
- proof, method, and limitations
- production owner and freshness date
- logical next step and qualification signal

Prefer a focused checklist, diagnostic, template, briefing, calculator, workshop,
or short guide over a broad ebook. Validate the title and promise with target
buyers before investing in elaborate production.

## Design the minimum path

```text
relevant promotion
  -> landing page with one promise and proportionate proof
  -> minimal form or direct access
  -> immediate delivery or download
  -> useful bridge to the next problem
  -> optional booking or reply path
  -> CRM ownership and follow-up
```

Use a video only when it explains the bridge more clearly than concise text.
Name a consultation or audit by its buyer outcome and set accurate expectations;
do not rebrand a generic sales call as free advice.

State capacity, availability, and deadlines truthfully. Never fabricate limited
weekly slots, demand, or urgency to make the booking step appear scarce.

Keep data collection proportionate. Explain what happens after submission and
separate asset delivery, marketing consent, tracking consent, and sales follow-up
where law or user expectation requires it.

## Build tool-independently

Any suitable landing-page, form, file-delivery, scheduling, CRM, and analytics
tool may implement the path. Choose based on:

- ownership and exportability
- privacy and consent controls
- accessibility and mobile behavior
- integration reliability and failure handling
- attribution needs
- editing and experiment workflow
- total operational cost

Do not make a course vendor or template a requirement.

## Verify the complete journey

Test as a first-time visitor on desktop and mobile:

1. source link, social preview, and campaign parameters
2. promise, proof, form labels, validation, and privacy information
3. submission, duplicate submission, and failure states
4. asset delivery, file access, and email timing
5. bridge copy or video and booking handoff
6. calendar timezone, availability, notifications, and confirmation
7. CRM record, source attribution, consent state, owner, and follow-up task
8. analytics before and after consent choices
9. accessibility, performance, and link integrity

## Promote with context

Use the profile, Featured proof, relevant posts, website, email signature, and
human conversations as distribution surfaces. Mention the asset in a message
only when it fits the conversation. Do not use a lead magnet as permission for
unsolicited bulk follow-up.

For a launch, test a small sequence of distinct angles rather than repeating the
same post. Examples include problem recognition, a case-derived lesson, a sample
from the asset, and an explicit offer.

## Measure and improve

Track:

- relevant source traffic
- landing-page completion
- successful delivery
- qualified consumption or response when measurable
- booking, show, and qualification rates
- opportunity and customer conversion
- complaints, unsubscribes, and consent failures

Find the narrowest leaking transition. Change one hypothesis at a time, define a
decision window, and preserve previous variants. Optimize for qualified progress,
not raw lead count.

## Quality Check

- Is the lead magnet independently useful to the intended buyer?
- Does the next step follow naturally without concealed intent?
- Is every form field operationally necessary?
- Can the team fulfill delivery and follow-up promises reliably?
- Are privacy, consent, tracking, endorsements, and email behavior reviewed for
  the applicable jurisdictions?
- Has the whole path been tested on mobile, with failures and consent variants?
